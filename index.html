About Malenadu Savi

We are passionate about preserving the rich culinary heritage of Malenadu. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring authentic, natural products from the heart of Karnataka's forests to your table.

Every jar of our forest honey is harvested sustainably from wild beehives, while our Amtekayi pickle follows traditional recipes passed down through generations. We believe in small-batch production, ensuring quality and authenticity in every product.

How It's Made

1

Sourcing

We source honey directly from wild beehives in pristine Malenadu forests, ensuring minimal environmental impact.

2

Harvesting

Traditional methods are used to extract honey, preserving its natural enzymes and nutritional properties.

3

Preparation

Small-batch processing ensures quality control, with careful attention to hygiene and traditional techniques.

4

Packaging

Products are carefully packaged in eco-friendly containers, ready to bring nature's goodness to your home.
